## 6. Consistencia y reconocimiento de errores

- **Umbrales del risk metric MOVIDOS cada ciclo** — **[20260614]**: *"when I made that video **I DCA below 0.5** and then last cycle **I DCA below point4** and then this cycle … **DCA below .3**"*; venta: *"Back then I would buy from 0 to 0.5 risk and then sell above 0.5 … **It was stupid now that I think about it** … I bought up to 0.4 risk … and I **didn't take profits until above .6**"*. - **Giro de 180° en tesis de ciclos:** 2021 "lengthening cycles" → 2026 "the four-year cycle did not die / strikes again". Nunca hace un post-mortem explícito de la tesis de 2021.
- **Sí reconoce errores puntuales:** [20230904]: *"the secondary Peak … did not go up as high and **it took me a couple months to really come to terms with that** admittedly"*. [20251230]: *"**Obviously I got some things wrong in 2025** … It's better to **pivot quickly and just admit you're wrong** … than to hold on and be stubborn to a narrative"*.
